Roxana's No Flour Banana Chocolate Bread

A sweet bread from a sweet sister

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of old fashioned steel oats
  • 4 or 5 very ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • A splash of olive oil
  • Cocoa powder (as much as you want)
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/3 cup of sugar (doesn’t come out too sweet so you can add more if you’d like sweeter)
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • A few drops of lemon juice
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 and line a bread pan with parchment paper.
  2. Blend all ingredients, except for chocolate chips, for about 10 seconds or untill well mixed.
  3. Fold chocolate chips into the batter.
  4. Add mixture to pan and bake for about 1 hr. Check to see if the center is done with a toothpick at 30 min and 10 minute increments after.
